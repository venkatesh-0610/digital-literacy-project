## 📖 Project Overview

This project was completed as part of the Digital Literacy course. The objective of this project is to understand and promote safe, effective, and responsible use of digital tools and platforms. As a Student Digital Ambassador, the project focuses on creating awareness among peers about digital literacy, professional online presence, communication etiquette, and cyber safety.

---

## 🧩 Tasks Summary

### 🔹 Task 1 – Digital Literacy Infographic

Generated a comprehensive, visually engaging one-page infographic using Canva. This infographic highlights the fundamental pillars of digital literacy, essential safe internet practices (like strong passwords and avoiding suspicious links), a curated list of useful digital tools for students, and guidelines for maintaining a professional online presence. The primary goal of this task was to distill complex digital well-being concepts into easily digestible visual content to share with peers effectively.

### 🔹 Task 2 – Digital Portfolio

Focused on building robust professional branding by creating and optimizing profiles across key networking platforms: GitHub, LinkedIn, and Kaggle. This task involved formatting academic and project experiences appropriately for each platform, highlighting technical skills, and understanding how to network effectively in a digital workspace. The ultimate outcome is a cohesive digital footprint that correctly demonstrates readiness for professional and academic opportunities.

### 🔹 Task 3 – Platforms Exploration

To interactively assess digital awareness, I successfully designed and developed a dynamic Digital Literacy Awareness Quiz utilizing Google Forms. This included formulating targeted questions centering on cyber hygiene and the practical usage of digital tools. Furthermore, I integrated Google Sheets to collect, seamlessly organize, and analyze the respondent data, gaining hands-on experience in fundamental data collection, analytics, and collaborative cloud platform capabilities.

🔗 Google Form Link: https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSciiv5-fqK89mR0Eye4vV964HNOcGEODGoHly6lMiyZLtxBZA/viewform?usp=publish-editor

### 🔹 Task 4 – Email Etiquette

Concentrated on mastering the principles of formal digital communication. I systematically drafted multiple professional emails tailored for standard academic and corporate scenarios (such as respectfully requesting extensions, submitting applications, and professional follow-ups). In conjunction with this, I compiled a structured, actionable checklist advocating for responsible social media usage—heavily emphasizing account privacy settings, respectful interaction norms, and critical thinking before posting.

### 🔹 Task 5 – Cybercrime Awareness

Delved deep into the critical area of online security by preparing an in-depth case study analyzing phishing attacks. This required researching exactly how social engineering tactics manipulate users and thoroughly evaluating real-world attack examples. Based directly on these specific findings, I constructed an extensive cyber prevention checklist designed to proactively educate users on quickly identifying red flags, independently verifying sources, and purposefully implementing robust security measures to promote a significantly safer online community.
