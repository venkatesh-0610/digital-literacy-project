# Do's 

1. Check Privacy Settings: Regularly review who can see your posts and photos. Keep personal stuff for friends only.

2. Think Before Tagging: Ask your friends before you tag them in photos or share their location.

3. Network Professionally: Follow companies and experts in your field (like Computer Science) to learn and find jobs.

4. Use Strong Passwords: Use different, hard-to-guess passwords for every app and turn on Two-Factor Authentication (2FA).

5. Support Good Causes: Use your voice to talk about things that matter, like sustainability or local community help.


# Don'ts 

1. Don't Post in Anger: Avoid "venting" or posting mean comments when you are upset. It can stay online forever.

2. Don't Share Fake News: Never forward news or "viral" alerts without checking if they are true (especially on WhatsApp).

3. Don't Over-Share: Avoid posting your home address, phone number, or travel plans in real-time.

4. Don't Bully or Troll: Never post hurtful comments or join "online fights." This can lead to serious legal trouble.

5. Don't Use Bad Language: Avoid using "slang" or rude words that could make you look unprofessional to a future employer.
